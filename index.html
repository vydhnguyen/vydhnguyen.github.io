About

Background

I am an operations-focused professional with experience supporting leadership teams through structured execution, program governance, and organizational systems.

My work sits at the intersection of project management, strategic operations, and executive coordination. I enjoy building clarity in complex environments, translating strategic goals into operational plans, and helping organizations execute effectively across teams.

Over the course of my career I have worked across functions such as compliance, finance coordination, HR operations, governance, and program delivery. These experiences have reinforced my belief that strong organizations are built through clear systems, disciplined execution, and thoughtful leadership.

More recently, I have become deeply interested in how artificial intelligence will reshape knowledge work and organizational structure. Rather than approaching AI purely from a technical perspective, I focus on its implications for operators and leaders - particularly how it may transform coordination, decision-making, and execution inside companies.

This website serves as a place to explore these ideas through projects and writing.
